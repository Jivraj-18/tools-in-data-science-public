# Course Structure & Assessment

## Course format

- **8 weeks**
- **Lecture + Lab** every week
- **Weekly topics** delivered by TAs
- **Hands-on labs** supported by TAs

## Assessment (as per proposal)

| Component | Details |
|---|---|
| Graded Assignments (GA) | Weekly topic questions on the ROE exam portal |
| 3 Projects | Project 1 after Week 3, Project 2 after Week 6, Project 3 at end |
| ROE Exam | Remote Online Examination covering the full course |
| Discourse Blog Posts (Non-Graded) | Weekly blogs; selected posts indexed on the portal |
| Hands On Lab (Non-Graded) | Students complete labs to learn and upgrade skills |
